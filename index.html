Vision

To be a trusted and responsive community-based hospital that delivers equitable, compassionate, and quality primary healthcare services, fostering healthier and more resilient communities

Mission

We are committed to providing accessible, safe, and patient-centered healthcare services through competent health professionals, standardized clinical care, and strong community partnerships. We aim to promote wellness, prevent illness, and ensure early diagnosis and basic treatment, while continuously strengthening our workforce, improving healthcare systems, and supporting maternal and child health. We uphold efficiency, integrity, and compassion in delivering essential health services, especially to underserved populations.

Innovation in Care

Why Choose Us?

Our proposed hospital in Mali-ao, Pavia, Iloilo offers accessible and affordable healthcare, quality and safe patient care, and the use of modern and efficient systems to improve healthcare delivery. We are committed to compassionate and patient-centered service through competent healthcare professionals, while promoting community engagement, disease prevention, and continuous excellence in meeting the healthcare needs of the community. We aim to bridge the gap in healthcare access by providing timely medical attention, preventive care, and compassionate treatment to underserved populations.

Through this initiative, we aspire to become a trusted healthcare institution that not only treats illnesses but also promotes wellness, dignity, and hope for every individual and family we serve.
